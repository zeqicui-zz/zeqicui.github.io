About


I like to call myself a multi-disciplinary developer and designer. I'm a highly self-motivated individual who takes action before most people. I take in consideration of not only the engineering aspects of my projects but also from a business, marketing and user experience side. The one rule that all my works follow is that making subjects practical and useful is not enough - it has to be beautiful as well.

I'm currently a student at the University of Waterloo majoring in Electrical Engineering. When I'm not studying or working, you can find me with my band covering the latest song from Coldplay.
